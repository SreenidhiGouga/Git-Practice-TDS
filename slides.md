# Algorithmic Complexity (Math)

Marp supports **KaTeX** for math rendering.

For an algorithm with nested loops:

$$
T(n) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} \sum_{j=1}^{i} 1 = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}
$$

Thus, the complexity is:

$$
O(n^2)
$$
